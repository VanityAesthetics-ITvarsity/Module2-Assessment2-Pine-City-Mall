"Fallen Angels" is a stylish and atmospheric Hong Kong crime drama directed by Wong Kar-wai. Known for its visually stunning cinematography and evocative soundtrack, the film explores the lives of hitmen, a mysterious woman, and a quirky mute in the neon-lit streets of Hong Kong. It weaves together multiple narratives of love, loss, and longing, creating a mesmerizing portrait of urban loneliness and existential angst. "Fallen Angels" is a must-watch for those who appreciate poetic storytelling and unconventional filmmaking that blends noir elements with profound emotional depth.

Fallen Angels

Cast: Leon Lai - Michelle Reis - Takeshi Kaneshiro - Charlie Yeung - Karen Mok

Age restriction: 16

Synopsis:

An assassin, his boss, an entrepreneur and two women cross paths in Hong Kong as their professional and love lives collide and influence each other, mostly without their knowledge.
