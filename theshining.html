"The Shining" is a psychological horror masterpiece directed by Stanley Kubrick, based on Stephen King's novel. Set in an isolated hotel during the winter, it follows Jack Torrance, a writer who becomes the winter caretaker with his family. As Jack's sanity unravels, eerie supernatural forces haunt the hotel, influencing him to commit violent acts. Known for its unsettling atmosphere, iconic imagery like the twins and the maze, and Jack Nicholson's unforgettable performance, "The Shining" is a chilling exploration of madness and terror that continues to captivate audiences decades after its release.>

The Shining

Cast: Jack Nicholson - Shelly Duvall - Danny Lloyd

Age restriction: 18

Synopsis:

Jack Torrance (Jack Nicholson) becomes winter caretaker at the isolated Overlook Hotel in Colorado, hoping to cure his writer's block. He settles in along with his wife, Wendy (Shelley Duvall), and his son, Danny (Danny Lloyd), who is plagued by psychic premonitions. As Jack's writing goes nowhere and Danny's visions become more disturbing, Jack discovers the hotel's dark secrets and begins to unravel into a homicidal maniac hell-bent on terrorizing his family.
